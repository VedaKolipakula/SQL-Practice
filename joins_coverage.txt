### LEFT JOIN

6. Identify employees whose department exists but the department does not have a manager assigned.

### RIGHT JOIN

7. List all departments and the employees assigned to them, including departments with zero employees.
8. Identify departments that currently have no employees assigned.

### FULL OUTER JOIN (or emulation)
